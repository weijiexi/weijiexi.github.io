About Weijie

Weijie works in graphic design, sculpture, and painting, moving from handmade mediums to digital art and interaction design. For many years, her goal was to become an interaction designer and a digital artist. Recently, she has focused on creative coding and has a passion for combining art, design, and technology. As a student pursuing an Art Practice degree at the University of California, Berkeley, Weijie is immersed in a vibrant, intellectually stimulating environment that encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and experimentation. This academic setting provides her with valuable resources and opportunities to further develop her creative coding skills and explore new artistic exploration avenues.
